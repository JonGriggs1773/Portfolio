My Projects

Pet Shelter

Pet Shelter is a full-stack MERN application that allows for animal shelters to keep logs on any animals coming and going from their shelter. This is meant to be a free, easy to use, and easy to implement platform for both animal shelters themselves, and people looking to adopt pets via an online portal.

Budget App

The Budget App, also known as the "Budget Miracle", is a Python/Flask/MySQL full-stack application built for people that need help budgeting their money. It utilizes algorithms based on the 50-30-20 budgeting rule to help you determine where your money should go on a monthly basis.

Team Manager

Team Manager is an application built using the MERN stack. This application was built to assist coaches, and teams in keeping track of their rosters and line-ups for upcoming games and events.
